Our Story

SuBrains Solutions Pvt. Ltd is a Technology Startup Company which has been started to bring the practical way of learning approach to the students and make them aware of the corporate working in tier 2 and 3 cities where we still have employment problems. We wanted to promote technological growth in India by building an Intelligent learning platform, with the help of Virtual Reality based learning in which we would like to enlighten young minds of India and make them adapt to technology in their graduation or under graduation levels so, with a mission of globalizing education we started SuBrains.
